Seevomap: Dynamic AI Research Knowledge Graph

Interactive visualization and analysis of AI capabilities across scientific domains

Explore Seevomap GitHub

What is Seevomap?

Seevomap is an interactive knowledge graph system designed to track and visualize the evolving landscape of AI capabilities in scientific research. As AI systems become increasingly capable across diverse scientific domains, understanding their strengths, limitations, and trajectories becomes crucial for researchers, practitioners, and policymakers.

The Challenge

The rapid proliferation of AI benchmarks and evaluation frameworks has created a fragmented landscape:

  • Hundreds of benchmarks across different domains with incompatible metrics
  • Difficult to compare model capabilities across different evaluation frameworks
  • No unified view of AI progress in scientific intelligence
  • Hard to identify gaps and opportunities for future research

Our Solution

Seevomap addresses these challenges by providing a unified, interactive platform that aggregates evaluation results from multiple benchmarks, visualizes them as an interconnected knowledge graph, and enables real-time cross-benchmark analysis. Users can explore AI capabilities by domain, task type, or model, identify trends, and track progress over time.

Key Features

Knowledge Graph Visualization

Interactive graph showing relationships between tasks, capabilities, and models

Smart Search

Find specific tasks, models, or capabilities with intelligent filtering

Leaderboard

Compare model performance across different scientific domains

Dynamic Updates

Real-time synchronization with benchmark results and new evaluations

Use Cases

For Researchers

Identify gaps in AI capabilities, find relevant benchmarks for your domain, and track state-of-the-art progress.

For Practitioners

Select the best model for your scientific application based on comprehensive capability analysis.

For Policymakers

Understand the current state of AI in science and inform evidence-based policy decisions.
