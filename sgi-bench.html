About SGI-Bench

SGI-Bench is a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating Scientific General Intelligence (SGI) — the capacity to navigate the iterative cycle of Deliberation, Conception, Action, and Perception with the versatility of a human scientist.

Deep Research

Evaluates literature review, information synthesis, and scientific reasoning capabilities

Idea Generation

Measures hypothesis formulation with effectiveness, novelty, detail, and feasibility

Dry Experiment

Tests computational experiment execution and code generation abilities

Wet Experiment

Assesses laboratory procedure planning and experimental protocol design
